RESPONSIBILITIES AT K-ELECTERIC

My Key Responsibilities at Karachi Electric are as follows:

  • Assisting in Safety Inspections

    Helping to inspect work areas and equipment to ensure they meet safety standards and identify potential hazards.

  • Maintaining Safety Records

    Organizing and updating safety-related documents, such as inspection reports, incident logs, and safety checklists.

  • Promoting Safety Awareness

    Supporting safety training sessions by distributing materials, setting up presentations, and reinforcing safety guidelines among staff.

  • Monitoring Safety Equipment

    Checking the availability and condition of safety equipment like fire extinguishers, first aid kits, and personal protective gear.

  • Reporting Incidents

    Assisting in reporting accidents or near-miss incidents and documenting the details for further analysis and corrective action.
