RESPONSIBILITIES AT ASIA LUBRICANTS

My Key responsibilities at Asia Lubricants are as follows:

  • Assisting in Production Planning

    Helping to schedule production activities and ensure resources, such as materials and manpower, are available as needed.

  • Monitoring Production Processes

    Observing and reporting on the efficiency of production lines to ensure everything runs smoothly and deadlines are met.

  • Maintaining Production Records

    Updating and organizing production logs, reports, and data to track daily output and any issues.

  • Coordinating with Teams

    Communicating with various departments, such as procurement and quality control, to align production activities with overall goals.
