RESPONSIBILITIES AT FALCON GLOBAL LOGISTICS

My Key responsibilities at Falcon Gobal Logistics are as under:

  • Overseeing Daily Operations

    Managing day-to-day logistics activities such as transportation, warehousing, and distribution to ensure smooth operations.

  • Coordinating with Teams

    Communicating and coordinating with drivers, warehouse staff, and other departments to ensure timely delivery and effective operations.

  • Monitoring Inventory Levels

    Keeping track of stock levels to ensure inventory is well-managed and replenished as needed.

  • Ensuring Compliance

    Making sure that logistics operations comply with company policies, safety standards, and government regulations.

  • Problem Solving

    Addressing and resolving any issues related to delays, transportation, or customer service that arise during the logistics process.
